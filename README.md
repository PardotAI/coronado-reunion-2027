# Coronado High School Class of 2007 - 20 Year Reunion

Welcome to the reunion website for Coronado High School's Class of 2007!

## About

This website serves as a central hub for our 20-year high school reunion celebration. It features:

- **Archived School Websites**: Direct links to Internet Archive snapshots of our school's website from 2003-2007
- **Reunion Information**: Details about the upcoming reunion event (coming soon)
- **Photo Gallery**: Share and view photos from our high school years (coming soon)
- **Where Are They Now**: Reconnect with classmates and see what everyone has been up to (coming soon)

## Archive Snapshots

The site links to 6 key snapshots of our school website from the Internet Archive (Wayback Machine):

- **Freshman Year** (Oct 2004)
- **Sophomore Year** (Apr 2005)
- **Junior Year End** (May 2006)
- **Senior Year Start** (Aug 2006)
- **Senior Year Spring** (Feb 2007)
- **Senior Year Fall** (Sep 2007)
